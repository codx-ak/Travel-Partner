What is Travel Insurance?

travel insurance policy is a versatile plan that offers financial compensation for a range of emergencies faced by the policyholder on a trip. It is a very effective way to secure any trip, given that you compare travel insurance plans before buying the best one for your upcoming trip.

Usually, people only learn about travel insurance when they need to visit a country which requires them to buy international travel insurance online. However, compared to pre-pandemic times, more travellers are now insuring their trips.

Coverage

  • Policy Extension: If for some reason your trip is extended and your flight is delayed for some days, then your overseas travel insurance will be extended for a fixed period.

  • Medical Expenses: If you or anyone travelling abroad with you is diagnosed with COVID-19 abroad, then your COVID-19 insurance for international travel will provide cover for these medical expenses & treatment.

  • Covers Lost Baggage: Flights may misplace your luggage or worse it may be stolen. Luckily, with an overseas insurance plan, you will get help with the costs of necessary personal expenses like clothing, medicines, and toiletries.

  • Medical Emergencies: If you need medical assistance due to an accident or sudden illness, your travel insurance from India will cover you. In fact, many travel insurance companies in India even offer cashless travel insurance. Through this, your insurer can settle the treatment expenses directly at the network hospital.

  • Adventure Sports Cover: This add-on will let you freely enjoy winter sports, adventure activities, etc. when you are on a trip abroad. Any medical expenses caused due to these activities will be taken care of, as per the travel insurance policy schedule.
